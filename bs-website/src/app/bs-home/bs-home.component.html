Hôtel Beausite

SPEND AN UNFORGETTABLE HOLIDAY IN A CHALET-HOTEL IN SWITZERLAND

Surrounded by the Alps , green meadows , towering cliffs and amidst the ancient tradition and genuine Swiss culture is romantic Adelboden Hotel Beau Boutique This luxury boutique hotel has 36 rooms, a beautiful restaurant with traditional cuisine and a spa and fitness area. In recent years it has become one of the best hotels in Adelboden , and even in the Bernese Oberland. The hotel offers a very special combination of traditional chalet spirit and modern comforts of a four star country house style.
